✨ Every entry you log is saved permanently to this artifact's database. The 7 starter categories (Trip, Clothes, Pub, Cafe, Dessert, Play & Fitness, Investments) show up as suggestions, but the category field accepts anything — type your own and it gets its own color, its own row in "By category," and its own slice of the trend chart. Matching is case-insensitive and trims spacing, so "gym", "Gym", and " gym " all land in one "Gym" category. Investments entries feed both the category total and their own type-by-type breakdown, based on whatever you type into "Investment type" (e.g. SIP, Stocks, Gold, FD). "This Year So Far" is your real, actual total for the current calendar year — it will show your true year-end number once December closes. The Yearly Projection is a separate estimate based on your all-time daily average (total logged ÷ days since your first entry × 365), meant to forecast ahead — it gets more accurate the longer and more consistently you log.
